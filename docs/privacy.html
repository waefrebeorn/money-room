🔒 Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 30, 2026

1. Information We Collect

We collect minimal information necessary to provide our services:

  • Account Data: Email address and payment information (processed through LemonSqueezy, we never store payment details).
  • Usage Data: API request logs (endpoint, timestamp, response size) for rate limiting and abuse prevention.
  • Wallet Addresses: Optional donation wallet addresses you provide for receiving payments.

2. How We Use Your Data

  • To provide and maintain our services
  • To enforce rate limits and prevent abuse
  • To comply with legal obligations (KYC/AML where applicable)
  • We do NOT sell your personal data to third parties
  • We do NOT use your data for advertising

3. Data Storage & Security

Account data is stored encrypted at rest. API keys are stored using environment variables with file permissions restricted to the running user (chmod 600). We use industry-standard security practices.

4. Third-Party Services

  • LemonSqueezy: Payment processing and subscription management
  • GitHub: Source code hosting
  • NousResearch: LLM API provider for agent reasoning

5. Data Retention

We retain usage logs for 90 days. Account data is retained until account deletion. You may request deletion of your data at any time by contacting us.

6. Your Rights

You have the right to access, correct, or delete your personal data. Contact us at eman56447@gmail.com for any privacy-related requests.
