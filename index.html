⚙️ Features

Every component is open source, documented, and free to use under MIT license.

🧬

10K Evolving Genomes

Darwinian evolution meets SGD. 11 trading parameters per genome, 18 learned feature weights, 4-float RNN hidden state.

📰

GDELT Global Feed

24 international news queries across 8 markets. Real-time lexicon sentiment scoring from global financial press.

📐

GAAD φ-Features

Golden-ratio interval analysis at φ, φ², φ³ scales. Multi-scale market decomposition from the GAAD-WuBu-ST paper.

🌊

DFT Frequency Analysis

Goertzel algorithm extracts dominant cycle from price history. Frequency-domain features for cycle-aware prediction.

🤖

Q-Learning Controller

Tabular Q-learning with ε-greedy exploration. Learns to select strategy weights from market state.

🧑‍🏫

10 Teacher Strategies

Independent daemon processes with unique personality filters. From ultra-conservative to degenerate, covering the strategy spectrum.
