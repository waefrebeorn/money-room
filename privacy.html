🔒 Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 29, 2026

1. Information We Collect

Money Room collects minimal information necessary to operate the service:

  • Public market data — Cryptocurrency prices, prediction market data, sports odds, and macroeconomic indicators from public APIs
  • Analytics — Basic page view statistics (no personal identifiers)
  • Payment data — If you subscribe to a paid tier, payment is processed by LemonSqueezy. We do not store credit card details.

2. Information We Do NOT Collect

  • No names, email addresses, or contact information (unless you contact us directly)
  • No IP address logging beyond standard web server logs
  • No cookies for tracking or advertising
  • No social media data
  • No location data

3. How We Use Information

Collected data is used solely to:

  • Operate and display the live market dashboard
  • Generate trading signals and predictions
  • Improve algorithmic models
  • Process paid subscriptions (via LemonSqueezy)

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or share personal information with third parties. Aggregate, anonymized market data may be shared through the public API.

5. Data Retention

Market data is retained for operational purposes. Personally identifiable information is retained only as long as necessary for the purpose collected.

6. Third-Party Services

Money Room uses the following third-party services:

  • LemonSqueezy — Payment processing for paid tiers
  • GitHub Pages — Website hosting
  • ESPN, Kraken, Yahoo Finance, FRED — Public data sources (no personal data shared)

7. Security

We implement reasonable security measures to protect data. However, no internet transmission is 100% secure.

8. Changes

This policy may be updated. Changes will be posted to this URL.
